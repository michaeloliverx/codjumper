Xbox 360 JTAG / RGH
- Copy the patch_mp.ff file to your cod4 game directory.
- Copy the codjumper.xex file to your console and set as a dashlaunch plugin.


Xenia
- Copy the patch_mp.ff file to your cod4 game directory.
- Copy the `plugins` directory to your Xenia directory.
- Change `allow_plugins` to `true` in your `xenia-canary.config.toml` file.
